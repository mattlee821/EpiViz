<h4>Is this the right plot?</h4>
Is a Circos plot the most appropriate type of plot for your data? You should consider whether your data would be better visualised by other method. In particular, if you have fewer than 50 variables <a href="https://nightingalehealth.github.io/ggforestplot/index.html">forest plots</a> are a good option. An alternative to Circos plots if you have lots of data are <a href="https://github.com/rainplots/rainplots">rainplots</a>.</p>
